Hardware Accelerated Computing

As Moore’s Law slows down, there is increasing interest in the use of hardware accelerators, especially for big data AI/ML workloads. Examples include Google’s Tensor Processing Units and the recent Inferentia from Amazon. The club works towards a big picture overview of the hardware accelerator landscape, and clear, in-depth and insightful coverage of design techniques for hardware accelerators (like those mentioned above) and the state-of-the-art in programmable logic based implementation of hardware acceleration.
